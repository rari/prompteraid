Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2025

PrompterAid respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we (do and do not) collect, how it's used, and how it's stored when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

PrompterAid does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. The only information retained is stored locally in your browser using local storage.

Specifically, we store:

  • Favorited Images: A list of images you've marked as favorites for each supported AI model (e.g., Niji 6, Midjourney v7).
  • Display Mode: Your preference for "Discord Mode" or "Website Mode," to ensure prompt formatting is consistent.

This information is stored only in your browser and is never sent to our servers or shared with any third parties.

2. Use of Information

The locally stored data is used solely to improve user experience by preserving your preferences across sessions. It is not analyzed, tracked, or shared.

3. Security

Because no personal data is transmitted to us, your data security relies entirely on your own device and browser environment. We do not use cookies, analytics scripts, or any server-side tracking tools.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
