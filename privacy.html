Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2025

PrompterAid respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we (do and do not) collect, how it's used, and how it's stored when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect

PrompterAid does not collect or sell your personal data for advertising or analytics purposes. We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide a personalized experience and save your preferences.

  • Favorited Images & Preferences: If you use PrompterAid without signing in, your favorites and settings are stored locally in your browser. If you choose to sign in with an account (via email or Discord), your favorites and preferences may be securely stored in association with your account, so they can be accessed across devices.
  • Display Mode: Your preference for "Discord Mode" or "Website Mode," to ensure prompt formatting is consistent.

Your account information (such as email or Discord ID) is used solely for authentication and to associate your preferences with your account. We do not share or sell this information to third parties.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used only to improve your experience by preserving your preferences across sessions and devices. It is not analyzed, tracked, or shared for marketing or analytics purposes.

3. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Account-based preferences are stored securely and are only accessible to you. We do not use cookies, analytics scripts, or any server-side tracking tools for marketing purposes.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
